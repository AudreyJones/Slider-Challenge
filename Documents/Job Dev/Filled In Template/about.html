About Me

As a neuroscience graduate walking the line between art and science, I have always had an interest in human-machine interactions and coding. This led me to pursue a full-stack developer role through the program at Flatiron School. Strong skills in Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, React/Redux, HTML/CSS, GIt/Github, RESTful routing, and PostgreSQL/SQL; familiarity with Java and Python. My goal is to help companies build tools that improve, inspire, and integrate well with modern life.
